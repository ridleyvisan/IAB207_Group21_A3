Mayday Australia Tour - Sydney & Melbourne

Live Nation & Chinatown Cinema Corp are proud to present Mayday's highly anticipated tour in Australia in 2023.
Australian based fans will again get a chance to witness the legendary Asian rock band as they make their way to Sydney's Qudos Bank Arena on Wednesday 10th May and Melbourne's John Cain Arena on Saturday 13th May.
Mayday are one of the most iconic bands in Asia, with a fan base that stretches far and wide. Known for their powerful vocals and electrifying performances, the band has captured the hearts of millions with their unique blend of rock, pop, and ballads. This will be Mayday's fifth time touring Australia, and fans can expect another night filled with high-energy music, stunning visuals, and unforgettable memories.
The band promises to bring their best to the stage, and fans can look forward to hearing their most beloved songs.With such high demand for tickets, fans are encouraged to act quickly to avoid disappointment. Don't miss this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to see Mayday perform live in Australia!
